DJIFlightControllerNoFlyStatusHeightLimited

Some no fly zones have several areas. A central area where no aircraft can fly or take off in, and then an intermediate area where flight height is restricted. This intermediate area can have a gradually relaxing height restriction as the aircraft moves further from the no fly zone center. If the aircarft is flying in this intermediate area, then the DJIFlightControllerNoFlyStatusHeightLimited enum will be used.
