Overview

The DJIBatteryState is used to keep track the real-time state of the battery.

fullChargeEnergy

Returns the volume (mAh - milliamp hours) of the total amount of energy stored in the battery when the battery is fully charged. The volume of the battery at full charge changes over time as the battery continues to get used. Over time, as the battery continues to get charged, the value of fullChargeEnergy will decrease.

@property (nonatomic, readonly) NSInteger fullChargeEnergy
