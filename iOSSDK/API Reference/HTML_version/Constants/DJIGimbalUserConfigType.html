DJIGimbalUserConfigType

Gimbal User Config. This is only supported by OSMO gimbal.

The gimbal has a feature called SmoothTrack SmoothTrack smooths out larger transitions in pitch and yaw (as opposed to keeping the camera pointed in the same direction at all times) SmoothTrack is used on two axes (yaw and pitch) and has three presets of fast, medium or slow which impact the tracking speed, deadband and acceleration settings. These settings can also be customized.
